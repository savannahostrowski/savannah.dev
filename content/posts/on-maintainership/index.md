I don't know what my job looks like in a year, two years or five years from now.

This is something that I've been thinking about a lot lately. We're in this weird squishy place where we are being told to move faster, automate more (or as much as possible), scale quicker, and do more with less. I suppose this is what we've always been told but lately, it feels more intense than ever. I think this is because of the economic climate, the rise of AI, and the fact that we're all just really tired. Every new product announcement, every new feature release, every new tool that promises to make our lives easier is met with a mix of excitement and exhaustion. We want to embrace the new, but we're also just so tired of trying to keep up.
